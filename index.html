CRAFT + COCKTAILS

OAKWOOD, LEEDS

Welcome to SALT Oakwood. Craft beer, cask ale and cocktails ‘on tap’ are served alongside burgers, wings and loaded fries. Open since Autumn 2022, we’re a family and dog-friendly venue on the edge of Roundhay Park. Join us from midday every day for our great food, drinks and atmosphere!

Drinks

We serve a diverse selection of locally brewed craft beers with something for everyone, from the craft curious to the connoisseur. We also have our famous cocktail creations available on tap, with 8 classic cocktails made with all-natural ingredients that are poured quickly without compromising quality or taste.

Drinks Menu

Food

When you visit SALT Oakwood, you’ll want to indulge in some exceptional comfort food courtesy of BigBuns, who run the kitchen. The independent burger brand never fail to deliver with their moreish dry-aged grass-fed patties, buttermilk wings and legendary loaded fries.

The menu is designed to perfectly complement the SALT craft ale collection. What better way to soak up a few pints of Jute IPA than a juicy double burger and some buttermilk wings? The whole menu is halal and also caters for vegans and vegetarians.
